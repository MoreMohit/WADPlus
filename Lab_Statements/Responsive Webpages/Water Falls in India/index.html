Athirappilly Falls (Kerala)

How to Reach Major Waterfalls:
  • Dudhsagar Falls: Take a train to Kulem Station, followed by a jeep safari or trek.
  • Jog Falls: Reach by road/train to Shimoga, then take a cab to Jog Falls.
  • Athirappilly Falls: 35 km from Kochi Airport, easily reachable by car/bus.
