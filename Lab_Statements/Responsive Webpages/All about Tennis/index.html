    Tennis Rules

    Each match is played as the best of 3 or 5 sets. A player must win 6 games by at least 2 games to win a set. The game includes scoring (15, 30, 40, game), faults, and tiebreakers.

    Grand Slam Schedule

    • Australian Open: January
    • French Open: May–June
    • Wimbledon: June–July
    • US Open: August–September
